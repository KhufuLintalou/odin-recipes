Pasta

Description

A simple, budget friendly pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodle
  • Salt and pepper

Steps by steps to make

  1. Boil water
  2. Throw in the desire amounts of pasta noodle
  3. Take out and put on a plate once it soften
  4. Sprinkle on as much salt and pepper as you like
  5. Enjoy i guess
